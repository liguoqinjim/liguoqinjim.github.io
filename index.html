Vagrant入门

@liguoqinjim · Sep 2, 2018 · 2 min read

Vagrant is a tool for building and managing virtual machine environments in a single workflow. With an easy-to-use workflow and focus on automation, Vagrant lowers development environment setup time, increases production parity, and makes the “works on my machine” excuse a relic of the past. Vagrant是一个在单一工作流中创建和管理虚拟机环境的工具。通过简单易用的工作流和对自动化的关注，Vagrant可以减少开发环境的配置时间，增加生产平价，并且使得”works on my machine”这个借口成为过去。
